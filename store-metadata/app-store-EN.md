## Description (≤4000)
```
NieruchomosciAI — Your AI assistant for buying and investing in Polish apartments.

The app fetches fresh listings daily from major portals (Domiporta, OLX), analyzes them with AI, and surfaces what listings don't tell you upfront:

• Fair-price label vs neighborhood median (below / fair / above + delta %)
• Red flags in description (missing land registry, area mismatches, inflated usable space)
• ROI calculator for investors — gross/net yield, payback in years, monthly cashflow
• Rent estimation based on district rates
• Top Investments ranking with filters: city, district, price, area, expected yield
• CSV export (Excel) for investors

WHO IT'S FOR

BUYERS (Free + Standard 39 PLN/mo)
- Daily listing matches based on your search criteria
- Push notifications for new listings matching filters
- Full red-flag list per listing (Standard)
- Map view with fair-price markers (Standard)

INVESTORS (Investor 149 PLN/mo)
- Everything in Standard
- ROI calculator with current mortgage rates
- Top Investments ranking by yield/payback/cashflow
- CSV export to Excel
- Priority support

HOW IT WORKS
1. Pick city, districts, price range, area
2. Daily push delivers best matches
3. Open listing — see fair-price, red flags, and (Investor) ROI
4. Tap „Open listing" → goes to original portal

DATA SOURCES
NieruchomosciAI aggregates public RSS feeds from real estate portals. We always link back to the original listing. We don't broker transactions and aren't responsible for listing content.

AI DISCLAIMER
Language models can hallucinate. Verify all analyses directly (notary, surveyor, appraiser). The app does NOT provide investment, legal or tax advice.

PRIVACY
GDPR-compliant. Passwords bcrypt-hashed. HTTPS only. Full privacy policy in-app + Settings → Delete account (30 days). Stripe handles payments — we don't store card data.

CONTACT
support@nieruchomosciai.pl
```
